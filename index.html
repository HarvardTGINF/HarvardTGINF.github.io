TGINF

Theory Graduate student and INdian Food (TGINF) is a student seminar at Harvard with a focus on the theoretical aspect of computer science. As the name suggested, there will only be theory grad students/post docs and indian food in TGINF. Well... recently there are some other types of food but nothing beyond. The seminar currently takes place on every Monday at 4:30pm-6pm ET with free food provided. Please add this to your calendar or email the organizer if you want to join in the mailing list!
